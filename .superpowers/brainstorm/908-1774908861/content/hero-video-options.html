A — Full Background Video

Video plays silently behind the hero text. Autoplay, muted, looped. Creates immediate atmosphere and visual energy. A dark overlay ensures text remains legible.

Cons

B — Featured Video Panel

Split hero: text on the left, video panel on the right with a play button. Video plays on click with sound. Works well if the video has a narrative or voiceover.

Pros

  • Sound-on viewing experience
  • Deliberate — visitor chooses to watch
  • Clean layout structure

Cons

  • Less immersive at first glance
  • Smaller viewport for the video
